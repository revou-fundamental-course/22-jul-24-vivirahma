The Houses

House Baratheon

Based in Storm's End, ruler of the Stormlands region. Their motto is "Ours is the Fury".

House Arryn

Based in The Eyrie, ruler of the Vale. Their motto is "As High as Honor".

House Lannister

Based in Casterly Rock, lord of the West. Their motto is "Hear Me Roar!" although it's better known by the phrase "A Lannister Always Pays His Debts".

House Targaryen

Based in Dragonstone, descendants of the old rulers of Westeros before the seizure of the throne. Their motto is "Fire and Blood".

House Martell

Based in Sunspear, the lord of Dorne. Their motto is "Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken".

House Stark

Based in Winterfell, ruler of the North. Their motto is "Winter is Coming".

In the world of Westeros, the choice of your house defines your destiny. Each house holds its own unique values, strengths, and challenges.
